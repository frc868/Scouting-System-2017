Team Information





Auton Instructions:

To show that a robot starts off with a ball or gear, click the respective check mark. To show the crossing of the baseline or the scoring of a gear, also click their respective checkmark.

To trace the robot's path during the Auton period, click on the marker button and use your mouse to show the path. To erase a path, simply click the clear button and click again on the field map.

If a robot makes a high goal during the Auton period, add how many balls they scored by the corresponding buttons.

If a robot socres points in the low goal, click the amount of balls that the robot initially had in it's position, then click the percentage of those balls actually made it into the goal.



Teleop Instructions:

If the robot was disabled, can collect balls, use a hobber or collect gears, mark so on their checkbox.

Mark how fast the robot was, (1) being slow and (3) being fast. click to add the gears scored by the robot during the match. Also mark if the robot was able to climb at the end.

If a robot socres points in the low goal, click the amount of balls that the robot initially had in it's posetion, then click the percentage of those balls actually made it into the goal.

If the robot scores a high goal during the match, click the position on the field, then click the corresponding button to show how many balls were scored.



Penalties Instructions:

If a robot commits any fouls or technicals, add the amount of each penalty to the respective group

If a robot gets a red or yellow card, or is ejected from the match, mark it on the corresponding checkbox.



Final Instructions:

On the Final tab, add any additonal notes or just any usefull data you collected while scouting. Also, once you are done with everything, submit your scout form.
